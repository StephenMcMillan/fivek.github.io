Freedom From Your Phone

FiveK runs independentaly on your Apple Watch meaning that you can leave your iPhone at home when you go out for a run. Your workout route and data is recorded to the Fitness app.

9 Week Programme

In just 9 weeks, you'll go from no running experience to comfortably running a 5K. You only need to run 3 times per week for an average of 30 minutes. Anybody can do it.

Improved Mental Wellbeing

Running does wonders for your mental wellbeing. Just 10-20 minutes a couple of times a week helps reduce stress and anxiety. You will feel better mentally and physically.
